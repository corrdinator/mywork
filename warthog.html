WARTHOG

Warthogs are wild members of the pig family found in grasslands and savannas of Africa. They are recognizable by their tusks, flat snouts, and warty facial bumps used for defense.

Warthogs are surprisingly fast runners and often seen kneeling to graze. Despite their rough appearance, they are peaceful grazers and often share burrows with other animals.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM:

Warthogs are kept in pen W17, close to the savanna trail path.
