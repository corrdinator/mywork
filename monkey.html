MONKEY

Monkeys are intelligent and social primates found in various regions, including tropical forests, savannas, and mountainous areas. They are known for their agility, playful nature, and curiosity.

There are two major types: Old World monkeys (Africa and Asia) and New World monkeys (Central and South America). Many species use their tails for balance, and some—like spider monkeys—even use them to grasp branches.
