PANDA

Giant pandas are beloved bears known for their black-and-white coloring and gentle personalities. Native to China, they mainly eat bamboo and can spend up to 12 hours a day feeding.

Though bears, pandas are solitary and quiet animals, with strong jaws and teeth for crushing bamboo. They are a global symbol of conservation due to their endangered status.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM:

Pandas are in pen P05, near the Zen garden and bamboo grove.
